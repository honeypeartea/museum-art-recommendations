We are graduate students from Johns Hopkins University. Looking back to our own college application, we always feel that the lack of information really brought us some unnecessary trouble, and sometimes, even anxiety. So, with the idea that information should be as reachable as possible for college applicants, this website was created. For now, we only support the records and predictions for 10 UC campuses but more are expected to come in the very near future. Hope you all find it useful.



Why Choose Us?

Most comprehensive admission records for UC since 1990. Advanced data mining model with high accuracy. Designed online certificates to improve your chances of admission. We promise to protect your personal information and it will only be available to yourself.
